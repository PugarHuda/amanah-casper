# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in the Amanah
project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project

Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments,
personal or political attacks, and publishing others' private information.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and in public spaces when
an individual is representing the project. As this project was built for the
**Casper Agentic Buildathon 2026**, contributors also adhere to the
[Casper Network Code of Conduct](https://casper.network/code-of-conduct).

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the maintainer
via the contacts in [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md) or on X [@BangDropID](https://x.com/BangDropID).
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1.
